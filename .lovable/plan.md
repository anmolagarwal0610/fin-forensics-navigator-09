

# Plan: Comprehensive Terms of Service Rewrite for FinNavigator AI

## Overview

Rewrite the Terms of Service page with comprehensive legal language that protects **Promarma Technologies LLP** from liability. The new terms will include AI-specific disclaimers, user responsibility clauses, limitation of liability provisions, and indemnification requirements - modeled after industry-leading AI SaaS companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, Palantir, etc.).

---

## Key Legal Protections to Add

### 1. AI Output Disclaimers
- Explicit statement that AI can produce errors, hallucinations, or inaccuracies
- User must independently verify all outputs before taking any action
- No guarantee of accuracy, completeness, or reliability

### 2. User Responsibility & Assumption of Risk
- User assumes full responsibility for how outputs are used
- User must have qualified professionals review outputs
- User acknowledges outputs are not legal, financial, or professional advice

### 3. Comprehensive Limitation of Liability
- Cap total liability at amount paid in prior 12 months
- Exclude all consequential, indirect, punitive damages
- Explicit exclusion of liability for AI-generated content

### 4. Strong Indemnification Clause
- User indemnifies company for third-party claims arising from their use
- Covers legal fees, damages, and settlements

### 5. Data & Input Responsibility
- User is responsible for legality and accuracy of uploaded data
- User must have authorization to upload all documents
- User warrants they have obtained necessary consents

### 6. Service Availability
- No uptime guarantees
- Right to modify, suspend, or discontinue service
- Maintenance windows and scheduled downtime

### 7. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution
- Specific jurisdiction (India for LLP)
- Mandatory arbitration clause
- Class action waiver

## Key Legal Language Highlights

### AI Disclaimer (Critical Section)
```text
THE SERVICE UTILIZES ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND MACHINE LEARNING 
TECHNOLOGIES WHICH ARE INHERENTLY PROBABILISTIC IN NATURE. THE 
OUTPUTS GENERATED BY THE SERVICE MAY CONTAIN ERRORS, INACCURACIES, 
OMISSIONS, OR HALLUCINATIONS. YOU ACKNOWLEDGE AND AGREE THAT:

(a) AI-generated outputs should be treated as preliminary analysis 
    only and not as definitive conclusions;
(b) You must independently verify all outputs through qualified 
    professional review before relying upon or acting on them;
(c) The Company makes no representations regarding the accuracy, 
    completeness, or reliability of any AI-generated content;
(d) You assume all risk associated with the use of AI-generated outputs.
```

### Limitation of Liability (Critical Section)
```text
TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY APPLICABLE LAW, IN NO EVENT 
SHALL PROMARMA TECHNOLOGIES LLP, ITS DIRECTORS, PARTNERS, EMPLOYEES, 
AGENTS, AFFILIATES, OR LICENSORS BE LIABLE FOR:

(a) Any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, punitive, or 
    exemplary damages;
(b) Any loss of profits, revenue, data, business opportunities, or 
    goodwill;
(c) Any damages arising from the use of or inability to use AI-generated 
    outputs;
(d) Any damages exceeding the amounts paid by you to the Company in 
    the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.
```

### Indemnification
```text
You agree to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Promarma Technologies 
LLP and its partners, officers, employees, and agents from any claims, 
damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable 
legal fees) arising from:

(a) Your use of the Service or AI-generated outputs;
(b) Your violation of these Terms;
(c) Your violation of any third-party rights;
(d) Any claims by third parties harmed by your use of the outputs.
```
